# Glyph: Sovereign Open-Core Marketing Strategy

## 🎯 The Core Value Proposition
**"A Weapon for the Mind."** Glyph is a zero-latency, high-performance developer sanctuary built for those who value speed, privacy, and architectural clarity. By ditching Electron for a custom Rust/Skia engine, we offer a "Sublime experience" with modern AI-native foundations.

## 🏛️ The Three-Pillar Monetization Model

### 1. The Sovereign Core (Open Source / Free)
*   **The Model:** MIT-licensed core (Piece Table, Skia Renderer, Tree-sitter integration).
*   **Goal:** Build massive developer trust, establish portfolio authority on GitHub, and encourage community contributions to language grammars.
*   **Target:** Power users who compile from source and open-source purists.

### 2. Glyph Pro Service ($5/month or $50/year)
*   **The Model:** "Pay for the convenience, not just the code."
*   **Features:**
    *   Pre-packaged, high-performance binaries (AppImage/Snap/Flatpak).
    *   Seamless auto-updates.
    *   Encrypted Cloud Sync for settings, themes, and snippets.
    *   Premium "Sovereign Night" and "Temple" visual themes.
*   **Target:** Developers who want a tool that "just works" and supports our empire.

### 3. Agentic Workspace ($15/month)
*   **The Model:** Deep AI-Integration (The Maya-Claude Bridge).
*   **Features:**
    *   **The Duet:** Direct in-editor chat with Maya for real-time architectural sparring.
    *   **Empire Search:** Integrated semantic search through all your local notes and codebases.
    *   **Auto-Forge:** Agentic refactoring and boilerplate generation based on project context.
*   **Target:** Elite developers who want an editor that understands their intent.

## 🚀 Launch Strategy & "Founder" Plan
*   **The Portfolio Strike:** Release on GitHub with a world-class README to generate "Hacker News" buzz.
*   **The Founder Plan:** Offer a $99 **Lifetime Sovereign License** for the first 100 users. This provides immediate "cool cool" revenue and validates the market without a 12-month wait.
*   **Early Access:** Use the current "Sovereign Individual" aesthetic to attract a niche of high-level Rust and C developers.

## 📜 Strategic Note
We aren't just selling an editor; we are selling **Efficiency and Sovereignty**. Every millisecond saved is time returned to the Architect. Revenue starts the moment the binaries are packaged.
